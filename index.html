Boston Latin School

Linguistics Club

Every Friday, Room 101

We learn about linguistics: the study of all languages as a whole, from how words are pronounced to the how culture influences the way we write. We solve problems such as translating Japanese Semaphore, writing cursive in Latin, or interpreting audio spectrograms, and then compete in the yearly NACLO competition.
